Project Overview

End Users:

My Low poly charcter is a Gundam. It is from a Science fiction anime called Gundam. My End Users are people who like Science Fiction and Gundam. It is also for children aged around 9-12, making it engaging and fun for younger audiences. The design is simple, so it's easy to print into toys or collectibles. Plus, it works great in animations for games and videos because of its smooth movements. This makes it perfect for both creative projects and play.

Requirements and specifications:

  • A functional skeleton for movement.
  • A clean and manageable model.
  • A low-poly model for easy animation.
  • Ensure low-poly geometry for animation efficiency and proper edge flow for smooth movements.
  • Realistic and visually appealing textures.
  • Good visual presentation
